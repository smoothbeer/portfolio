Overview

A digital World Cup 2026 sticker book where you collect players by dragging images from Google Images into the album. You open packs to get assigned players, then go and find the photos yourself. The search is the collecting experience. 48 teams, 1,440 slots, all stored locally in the browser.

View project →
The Idea

Google Images is the largest image library in the world but there's no real reason to browse it. The sticker book gives you one. Each pack assigns you a handful of players to hunt down and the collecting mechanic makes each find feel worthwhile. The album provides the structure, the open web provides the content.

Pack Economy

Packs gate the experience so collecting plays out over days rather than minutes.

Mechanic Detail
Pack size 6 cards
Starting packs 10
Replenish 1 every 15 min, up to 4 per hour, capped at 10
Rare pull rate 5% per player card
Duplicates Upgrade to rare or store. 3 dupes convert into a bonus pack
Seeded dupes Packs 3 and 5 guarantee a duplicate to introduce the mechanic early
