Overview

Thingdoms is an experimental art project released in 2021 as a complete collection of 10,000 digital characters. It explores identity, mood, and narrative through character-based generative systems.

Conceived as a kind of Gesamtkunstwerk, it emerged during a moment shaped by generative art, blockchain, and internet culture, and lives across various mediums.

The Collection

Over time, the original characters have been reimagined and released as additional 10,000-piece collections, each in a new style.

Storage

Each artwork exists as an ERC-721 token on the Ethereum blockchain, with the images and metadata stored on IPFS, a distributed storage protocol. This means the work lives permanently outside any single platform or server. All versions of the collection are archived the same way.

Licence

Collectors can display, adapt, and build on their Thing as part of their own creative practice. The licence is designed to encourage participation rather than restrict it, while keeping the authorship and trademarks with the artist.
