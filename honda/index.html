Overview

A social-first pre-launch campaign for the Honda HR-V, built around a single observation: nobody actually parks in their garage. It's full of everything else.

The brief positioned the HR-V as a car that fits into your life. We took that literally — and made the mess the message.

The Work

Perfect Fit Garage played on the gap between how people imagine their garage and how it actually looks. The content ran across social as a pre-launch activation, turning the everyday chaos into a reason to care about the car before it even hit showrooms.
