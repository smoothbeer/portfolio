Overview

A brand manifesto and identity refresh for GOAL — the global football media platform. Produced through VICE Media Copenhagen, the brief called for a new voice, visual direction, and narrative framework that could carry the brand forward.

The project led directly to a full-time position at Footballco, where I went on to build editorial frameworks, social formats, and long-form storytelling systems.

The Work

Nine slides. One argument. The manifesto reframes football as something far bigger than a sport — opening with a provocation and building through a sequence of cultural, emotional, and social claims before landing on a single definitive sign-off.
