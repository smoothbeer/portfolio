Overview

Global platform launch for DAZN, the sports streaming service. The brief was to establish the brand around a single idea — Raw Sport — and roll it out across every touchpoint: broadcast idents, fight promos, social content, and outdoor campaigns in markets worldwide.

Part of a core creative team at Dentsu McGarryBowen, I worked across concept, art direction, and copy — helping define the visual language and editorial tone that gave the platform its identity.

The Work

The output spanned 360 — broadcast, social, OOH, and print — across global markets. In any given week the team would be cutting fight promos, building season-launch campaigns, and producing platform idents simultaneously.

Design System

The visual identity was built as a toolkit — a system flexible enough to work across sports, markets, and formats while keeping the brand unmistakable. Developed alongside the DAZN in-house team and the Dentsu creative department, it gave local teams the tools to produce on-brand content at speed.
